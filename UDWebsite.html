Warhammer 40k is a fantasy setting were Humanity has progressed into the Year 40,000, where the god emperor of Mankind sits on his golden throne. In the Grim Darkness of the far furture there is only war.

The God Emperor, enshrined in his throne, is besiged on all sides by Xenos, Herotics, and Traitors. So he sends forth his angles, The Adeptes Astartes, Space Marines.
